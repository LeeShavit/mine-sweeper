Welcome to Minesweeper! 🎉

Objective: Uncover all the safe squares and avoid the mines! Your mission is to reveal every cell that doesn’t have a mine while staying clear of any hidden dangers.

Basic Controls:

Left Click 🖱️: Reveal what’s inside a cell.

Right Click 🚩: Flag or unflag a suspected cell (flagged cells can’t be revealed).

Game End Conditions:

LOSE 💥: Click on a mine, and the game reveals all mines. You lose and the game ends.

WIN 🎉: Flag all the mines and reveal all other cells to win the game!

Expanding Cells:

When you left-click a cell, one of these happens:

  • MINE 💣: The mine is revealed, and you lose a life. Oh no!
  • Cell with Neighbors 🔢: The cell shows a number indicating how many mines are around it.
  • Cell without Neighbors 🌟: The cell and its neighboring cells are automatically revealed.
Lives ❤️:

You have 3 Lives. Clicking on a mine loses you a life. Keep an eye on your lives and play carefully!

Hints 💡:

You have 3 Hints. Click a hint to reveal a cell and its neighbors for a few seconds. The hint will then disappear. Use them wisely!

Safe-Clicks 🛡️:

You have 3 Safe-Clicks. Clicking this button will briefly mark a random cell that is safe to click. Handy for those tricky spots!

Manual Mine Placement 🛠️:

Create your own game! Switch to “Manually Create” mode to place mines yourself. After placing all the mines, start the game and enjoy!

Mega-Hint 🌟:

Use it once per game. Click any two cells to reveal the area between them for 2 seconds. It’s a great way to get a sneak peek!

Exterminator Button 🚀:

Eliminate 3 Mines. Clicking this button will randomly remove 3 mines from the board. A powerful tool to help you out!

Have a blast and good luck finding all the safe squares while avoiding the mines! May your Minesweeper adventure be full of fun and excitement! 🌟🎮🕹️
